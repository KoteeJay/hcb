About Us

The Human Capacity Building (HCB) Program, a collaborative initiative between Valour Consult and the Akwa Ibom State Government, is poised to revolutionize the landscape of youth empowerment in the state. With a mission to equip a minimum of 15,000 youths with invaluable skills, the program stands as a beacon of opportunity and progress. Structured into three pivotal components, the HCB Program offers comprehensive training in:

  • HSE (Health, Safety, and Environment): Covering levels 1, 2, and 3, this segment ensures participants are adept in essential safety protocols, fostering a culture of workplace security and awareness.
  • Agro Training: Delving into both crop and animal production and management, this facet empowers individuals with the expertise to thrive in the agricultural sector, a cornerstone of sustainable development.

  • Tech Skills: Embracing the digital era, participants are immersed in a diverse range of technological disciplines including product design, digital marketing, data analytics, and frontend web development, positioning them at the forefront of innovation and industry demand.
Commencing from Nsit Ubium Local Government Area (LGA), the program spans an immersive duration of 3-4 weeks, ensuring intensive and impactful learning experiences with world class certifications Prospective candidates within the LGA are encouraged to register through the program's dedicated website at HCB9ja.com.
